\begin{lemma}[Routh-Hurwitz criterion]
	The third order polynomial $P(z) = z^3 + a_2 z^2 + a_1 z + a_0$ has all its roots in the negative half-plane if and only if $a_0, a_1, a_2 > 0$ and $a_2 a_1 > a_0$
\end{lemma}
